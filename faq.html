Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about documentation and our services.

What documents do I need to start a business in Timor-Leste?

The requirements vary based on the type of business (sole proprietorship, Lda, etc.), but typically include a business plan, identification documents for directors, a registered address, and capital proof. We guide you through the entire SERVE registration process.

How long does it take to obtain a work visa?

Processing times can fluctuate significantly depending on the government's current workload and the completeness of the initial application. Generally, after submission, expect a waiting period of 4 to 8 weeks. We strive to expedite the preparation phase to minimize delays.

Can TimorDocs assist local Timorese citizens with civil documents?

Absolutely. We frequently help Timorese citizens with obtaining copies of birth, marriage, and death certificates, as well as assistance with national identification card processes and tax registration.

Are your fees inclusive of government charges?

No. Our fees cover our professional service and consultation. Government fees, stamp duties, and official processing charges are paid separately by the client directly to the respective institutions or reimbursed to us at cost. We provide a clear breakdown of all expected costs beforehand.
